ABOUT US

Who Are We?

Autobots is a wing of M/s.Mayura's Industrial Services (MIS) which has a rich experience of over 20 years in the Indian Automation Industry. Mayuras is Galaxy System Integrator for M/s. Schneider Electric & distributor for M/s.Bonfiglioli for the past 20 years. We have the pleasure of introducing Doosan robotics in India this year.

What Do We Have?

We have with us a wide range of products in our portfolio to meet the needs of all customers.

6-Axis Cobots

Advanced 6-axis collaborative robots by Doosan Robotics designed for precision and safety.

Desktop Robots

Compact desktop robots perfect for education and light industry applications.

Production Line Accessories

Accessories to simulate and enhance production line efficiency and automation.

Collaborative Robots

Robots designed to work alongside humans safely and efficiently in various environments.

Wide Range of Grippers

Versatile grippers for different applications and precision requirements.

Simulation & Control Software

Advanced robot simulation and control software for optimal performance.

What Can We Do For You?

Committed to do our part in achieving "Skilled India", we seek to combine our expertise in the industry and emerging technologies in the market to benefit our customers.

At AUTOBOTS, our vision is to meet the current skill demand, anticipate tomorrow's needs and to equip students & industries accordingly.
